## Working Alignment Discussion

### 1️⃣ Impact & Focus
- Where do you think you create the most impact today?
- What drains your energy?
- When did you feel most effective in the past year?

### 2️⃣ Strategic Context
- Where do you feel you lack context or visibility?
- What decisions in the past year felt unclear or abrupt?

### 3️⃣ Working Style & Communication
- What helps you do your best work?
- How do you prefer to receive feedback?
- When do you typically need more direction vs more autonomy?

### 4️⃣ Growth & Direction
- If we fast-forward 12 months, what would make you feel proud?
- What capabilities do you want to strengthen this year?

### 5️⃣ Expectations 
- What do you expect from me as your manager?
- What does good support from me look like?
